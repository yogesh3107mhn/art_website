Why drawing at create art

Learn from real artists

Our drawing curriculum is designed to nurture both technical accuracy and creative confidence. Students work with graphite, charcoal, pastels, and ink — exploring still life, portraiture, nature study, and imaginative illustration.

✦ Small batch size (max 8 students)

Individual attention, regular feedback, and portfolio development for competitions & school art exhibitions.

Detailed curriculum

What you'll learn

  • Fundamentals: Line, shape, form & spatial awareness
  • Shading techniques: Hatching, cross-hatching, blending
  • Perspective drawing: 1-point, 2-point & atmospheric
  • Still life composition & texture rendering
  • Human figure & portrait basics (proportions)
  • Charcoal & expressive mark-making
  • Nature studies: Leaves, flowers, animals
  • Light & shadow: Chiaroscuro practice
  • Sketching from life & memory drawing
  • Creative illustration & storytelling through art
  • Portfolio preparation for inter-school contests
  • Final exhibition piece development
