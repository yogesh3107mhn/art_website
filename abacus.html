Why abacus at create art

Train the brain beyond numbers

Abacus learning stimulates both left and right hemispheres of the brain, leading to improved memory, faster mental calculation, and enhanced problem-solving skills. Our certified instructors follow a proven 8-level curriculum that makes math joyful.

✦ Scientifically designed program

Small group classes (max 6 students) ensure individual attention. Regular progress tests, speed drills, and monthly competitions.

Course structure

What your child will master

  • Introduction to Abacus: Parts, values, and basic finger techniques
  • Addition & Subtraction using direct formulas
  • Combination formulas (Friends, Families, Pairs)
  • Mental visualization of abacus (image calculation)
  • Multiplication & Division on abacus
  • Speed building: Timed drills & flash anzan
  • Decimals, fractions & advanced arithmetic
  • Brain gym exercises for concentration
  • Listening & recall exercises (dictation)
  • Mock championships & state-level preparation
  • Certification after each level (8 levels total)
  • Final project: mental math marathon
